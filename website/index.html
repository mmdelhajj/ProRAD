Features

Everything your ISP needs

A complete platform to manage subscribers, monitor your network, and grow your business.

Subscriber Management

Create, edit, and manage thousands of subscribers with bulk operations, status tracking, and automated expiry handling.

Live Bandwidth Monitoring

Real-time traffic graphs per subscriber using MikroTik Torch. Monitor upload/download speeds, latency, and per-connection breakdown.

Automated Billing

Invoices, prepaid cards, reseller balance management, and automated renewal. Track revenue and financial reports in real-time.

RADIUS & PPPoE

Full RADIUS server with MS-CHAPv2 and PAP authentication. Change of Authorization (CoA) for instant speed changes without disconnect.

Enterprise Security

Hardware-bound licensing, encrypted backups, anti-debug protection, LUKS disk encryption, and multi-point license validation.

Multi-Tier FUP

3 daily and 3 monthly Fair Usage Policy levels with automatic speed reduction. Free Hours quota discounts and time-based bandwidth rules.

Platform

Built for scale, designed for simplicity

From 100 subscribers to 30,000+ — ProxRad grows with your ISP.

MikroTik Integration

Deep integration with MikroTik RouterOS. Manage queues, monitor traffic, run diagnostics, and apply speed changes — all from one panel.

  • ✓ Real-time Torch bandwidth monitoring
  • ✓ Automatic queue management (PCQ, Simple)
  • ✓ IP pool auto-import and management
  • ✓ Ping, traceroute, and DNS lookup from router
  • ✓ CoA for instant speed changes
